Background

Recently, an app on my phone updated and started displaying a year progress bar.

I was surprised by the impact of seeing we were already 33.1% through a year where I had, so far, achieved little.

Perception of time had already changed during the Covid-19 lockdown; days could easily become identical and slip away. Time passed by relentlessly.

I needed a tiny web project to quickly get myself back on track and find the satisfied feeling of having completed something, anything.

What if that anything could help me claw back productivity from the time that was slipping away and continue to do so on an ongoing basis?

This is no sophisticated system like GTD but rather a succinct and calming presentation of the terrifying reality that is time slipping away and things I promised myself I'd do.

I use this as one of a handful of tabs I always have open along with my gmail, calendar etc.

Questions

Where is my data stored? I use "localStorage" which stores it in your web browser, on your own computer and not in the cloud.

So is my data safe? If you shut down your browser, or your computer, your data will be there when you next view the website from that browser on that computer. If you uninstall the browser or use some cleaning software, then you should backup the data first.

So is my data secure and private? Anyone who has access to your computer could see your data.

How do I backup my data? I use "print to PDF" which creates a small file that serves as a rudimentary backup.

What tech did you use? CSS grid and javascript, using github and netlify for hosting (thanks for the free tier!).

You can customise certain things eg what day you prefer the week to start.


Day Settings

You can choose to calculate day progress of the whole day (just put 0 for start hour and 24 for end hour) or you may prefer to use your working hours (eg. 9 for start and 17 for end). For now, we can't control minutes.






You can choose what your progress message says. By using the word today or percentage in angle brackets, ie. <today> and <percentage> you can display today's date and progression of the day





Week Settings

You can choose to calculate week progress of the whole week (put the week duration as 7), or work week (eg duration of 5) and have the week start on any day. You need to use a number for the day, where 0 is Sunday, 1 is Monday, 2 is Tuesday, 3 is Wednesday, 4 is Thursday, 5 is Friday and 6 is Saturday:





You can choose what your progress message says. By using the word weekbegin or percentage in angle brackets, ie. <weekbegin> and <percentage> you can display the week begin date and progression of the week





Month Settings

You can choose what your progress message says. By using the word month or percentage in angle brackets, ie. <percentage> you can display the progression of the month or the month name.





Year Settings

You can choose what your progress message says. By using the word year or percentage in angle brackets, ie. <percentage> you can display the progression of the year or the year itself.
